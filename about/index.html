Albums are extraordinary; with just a seemingly simple collection of tracks, artists manage time and time again to capture our hearts and minds with records that are somehow much more than the sum of their parts. This inspired Barrowclift siblings Marc & Michael to reflect on (and borderline obsess over) some such albums from their past and present. How about you join us? Plug in, start Track 1, and let's explore together.
